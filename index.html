Who Is Mira?


Mira is a NYC-based young influencer in the performing arts. She stays on the pulse of urban culture.

As a High School Freshman, education is extremely important to Mira and is her primary focus.
She is a straight A student who dedicates all free time and spare moments to MUSIC and ENTERTAINING which makes her happy!
Mira has been dancing from the time she was able to stand and has been a member of the Pink Diamonds ♦ Championship Dance Step Team.

She is quickly becoming a sought after entertainer/instructor and has had the opportunity to showcase her talents at events across NYC including the UniverSOUL Circus® tour & World Trade Center memorial celebration.

If that's not enough, Mira is also an avid competitive tennis player who's won the title Queen of the Court on many occasions.
